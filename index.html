Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI marketing engine

How quickly will I start seeing results?

Most of our clients start seeing qualified leads within 3-7 days of activation. Our AI system immediately begins identifying and reaching out to prospects in your target market. Some clients report seeing their first leads within 48 hours. The exact timeline depends on your industry, location, and the specificity of your target audience, but we've optimized our system to deliver results as quickly as possible. We provide detailed analytics and reporting so you can track every lead generated.

Do I need to hire a salesperson to manage the leads?

Not necessarily. Our system handles the initial lead capture and follow-up automatically, so you don't need a dedicated sales team. However, you will need someone to close the deals and manage client relationships. Many of our clients handle this themselves or delegate it to existing team members. The beauty of our system is that it does all the time-consuming prospecting and initial engagement work, so your sales staff can focus entirely on closing deals and serving customers. This dramatically improves productivity and allows you to grow without proportional increases in headcount.

How does the AI learn about my specific business?

During the onboarding process, we gather detailed information about your business, services, target audience, value proposition, and ideal customer profile. We also analyze your website, past customer data (if available), and market positioning. Our AI then uses this information to understand your unique selling points and craft personalized outreach messages. The system continuously learns and improves by analyzing which leads convert best and adjusting its approach accordingly. You'll also have access to our platform where you can provide feedback and refine targeting parameters at any time.

Is this just spam or unsolicited outreach?

Absolutely not. Our system is designed to identify people who are actively searching for solutions in your industry and engage them with relevant, personalized messages. We comply with all anti-spam regulations including CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and local regulations. Our outreach focuses on providing genuine value and solving real problems for prospects. The AI analyzes engagement patterns and only continues conversations with people who show genuine interest. We maintain strict data privacy standards and never use aggressive or deceptive tactics. The result is higher quality leads with better conversion rates because we're reaching people who actually want to hear from you.

What's the pricing and how does it work?

We offer flexible pricing models to fit businesses of all sizes. Our standard plans are based on the number of leads generated or a monthly subscription with a guaranteed minimum number of leads. We also offer performance-based pricing where you only pay for qualified leads that meet your specific criteria. There are no hidden fees, setup costs, or long-term contracts required. Most clients see ROI within the first 30 days. During your free consultation, we'll analyze your business and provide a customized quote based on your goals, industry, and target market. We're confident in our results and happy to discuss pricing options that work for your budget.
